Orange Chicken

Best Orange Chicken you'll have!!

This 4 ingridient orange chicken made with frozen chicken tenders is great for when you're craving takeout. It's so simple to make at home, is very budget-freindly, and only takes 4 ingridients. I serve it over rice, topped with green onions and sesame seeds.

Ingridients include:

  • 1 cup of marmalade
  • 1/2 cup kansas city style BBQ
  • 1/4 cup low sodium soy sauce
  • 1(1 pound) bag frozen fully cooked chicen nuggets
  • slized green onions(optional)
  • sesame seeds

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 f (200c). Place frozen nuggets in a single layer on a baking sheet.
  2. Bake in the preheated oven until hot and crispy, 11 to 13 minute, or according to package directions.
  3. Meanwhile, whisk marmalade, BBQ sauce and soy sauce together in a small saucepan and heat over low heat until hot, about 5 minutes.
  4. Place nuggets in a large bowl. Drizzle sauce over the top. Toss to coat.
